About Homelet Veggies

Our Story

Homelet Veggies was born from a passion for sustainable farming and community wellness. Nestled near Moiben Town, we grow a wide variety of fresh vegetables using eco-friendly and organic practices. Our journey started as a small family farm and has grown into a trusted provider for both households and businesses across Kenya.

Our Mission

We aim to nourish our communities with clean, healthy food while protecting the environment. Every vegetable we harvest is a reflection of our love for the land, our people, and the future.
